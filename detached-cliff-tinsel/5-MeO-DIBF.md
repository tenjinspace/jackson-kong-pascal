# 5-MeO-DIBF

5-meo-dibf

## Basic Information

**Summary**

An uncommon and new psychedelic drug related to tryptamine; the Benzofuran analogue of 5-MeO-DiPT. Probably a serotonin receptor partial agonist. Little history of human use.
