# 4,4-DMAR

4,4-dmar

## Basic Information

**Summary**

Very closely related stimulant to Aminorex and Pemoline. Was sold as a designer drug for quite awhile under the brand name "Serotoni" has been linked to at least 31 deaths. It is a potent SNDRA.
