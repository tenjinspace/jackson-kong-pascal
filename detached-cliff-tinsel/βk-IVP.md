# βk-IVP

bk-ivp

## Basic Information

**Summary**

The beta-ketone analogue of IVP, this is a rare RC stimulant in the same family as MDPV. Little is known but effects are probably similar to MDPV or more generally amphetamine or high dose caffeine.
