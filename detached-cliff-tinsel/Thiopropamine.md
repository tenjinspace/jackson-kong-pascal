# Thiopropamine

thiopropamine

## Basic Information

**Summary**

A substance that is closely related to Methiopopramine (MPA). Is roughly about 1/3rd the potency of amphetamine, differs from amphetmine from the phenyl ring is switched with a thiophene ring.
