# 4-Fluoromethylphenidate

4-fluoromethylphenidate

## Basic Information

**Summary**

A relatively uncommon, more serotonergic RC derivative of methylphenidate. A dopamine reuptake inhibitor possessing similar effects to associated stimulant compounds.
