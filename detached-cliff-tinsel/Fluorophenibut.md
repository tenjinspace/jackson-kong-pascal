# Fluorophenibut

fluorophenibut

## Basic Information

**Summary**

An anxiolytic analogue of GABA and Phenibut, with similar activity to the latter. Expected to be 5-10 times as potent as phenibut, some preliminary reports suggest tolerance builds more slowly.
