# 4-CHLORODIAZEPAM

4-chlorodiazepam

## Basic Information

**Summary**

A benzodiazepine derivative of Diazepam, yet lacks affinity for GABA(a) receptors, yet is still a sedative, that has pro-convulsant effects. Yet at lower doses it has been proven to be neuroprotective.
