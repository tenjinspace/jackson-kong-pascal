# 4-AcO-MiPT

4-aco-mipt

## Basic Information

**Summary**

A quite potent tryptamine that has be related to having "shroom-like" visuals. Yet without much bodyload. Very hydroscopic. Seems to be quite safe in dosing. Similar to the likes of 2C-B.
